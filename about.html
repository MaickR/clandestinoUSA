Our Spanish Heritage

The Clandestino Story: Bringing Authentic Spain to Mount Shasta

Born from a passion for authentic Spanish cuisine and hospitality, The Clandestino USA brings the warmth and flavors of Spain to the heart of Mount Shasta. Our journey began with a simple vision: to create an intimate space where traditional tapas, curated wines, and Mediterranean culture could flourish.

  • Authentic Spanish Ingredients

    We source the finest charcuterie, cheeses, and conservas directly from Spain, ensuring every bite transports you to the Mediterranean.

  • Curated Wine Collection

    Our sommelier-selected Spanish wines pair perfectly with our tapas, featuring both renowned and hidden gem producers from across Spain's wine regions.
