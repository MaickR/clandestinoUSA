Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about reservations, our menu, Wine Club, and more.

Making a reservation is easy! You can contact us via WhatsApp at +1 408-609-0027, call us directly, or use the contact form above. We recommend booking in advance, especially for weekends and special events. Our team responds promptly during business hours (2:00 PM - 8:00 PM daily).

The Clandestino specializes in authentic Spanish tapas and Mediterranean cuisine. Our menu features traditional dishes from various regions of Spain, including Andalusian tapas, Basque pintxos, and classic paellas. We pride ourselves on using high-quality ingredients and traditional preparation methods to bring you the true flavors of Spain in Mount Shasta.

Our Spanish Wine Club offers two exclusive membership tiers: Diamond ($149/quarter) featuring 4 premium Spanish wines from renowned regions, and Gold ($99/quarter) showcasing 4 exquisite Aragonese wines. Members receive curated selections, tasting notes, pairing suggestions, and exclusive invitations to wine events. Learn more about membership benefits.
